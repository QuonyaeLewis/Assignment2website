ELCTRONICA APPLE iMAC

APPLE iMAC

Designed to fit perfectly into your life, the 2021 Apple iMac has been created with a 23.5” 4.5K display that's only 11.5mm thin, Apple's powerful M1 chip with 8‑Core GPU, and 8GB RAM. Thanks to the variety of colour options, you can be sure your new iMac will make a statement wherever you place it. All-in-One computing The iMac's All-in-One design means you're supplied with everything you need to get up and running – a mouse, keyboard and of course the computer itself, which is hidden away inside the same slender case as the monitor. Helpfully, it's contemporary and compact design ensures computer won't take up too much room.
