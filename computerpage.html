ELCTRONICA COMPUTER PAGE

iMAC

Designed to fit perfectly into your life, the 2021 Apple iMac has been created with a 23.5” 4.5K display that's only 11.5mm thin, Apple's powerful M1 chip with 8‑Core GPU, and 8GB RAM. Thanks to the variety of colour options, you can be sure your new iMac will make a statement wherever you place it. All-in-One computing The iMac's All-in-One design means you're supplied with everything you need to get up and running – a mouse, keyboard and of course the computer itself, which is hidden away inside the same slender case as the monitor. Helpfully, it's contemporary and compact design ensures computer won't take up too much room.




HP STREAM

Stay up to date with social media, the latest news headlines and gripping box sets with the HP Stream 11 Laptop. Powered by an Intel® Celeron™ processor, your laptop will be able to handle your everyday routine. Operating system Windows 10 S with a 1 year subscription to Microsoft 365.




MICROSOFT SURFACE

Microsoft's Surface has the power of a laptop in a lightweight, versatile form. A 13" touchscreen and 8GB RAM help you create, relax and share without missing a beat, and the 120Hz refresh rate ensures everything on-screen feels super-fluid and responsive. The integrated Kickstand lets you set up wherever's most comfortable, with two 1080p cameras making video conferencing a cinch. It'll provide all the power, portability and productivity you need to make the most out of Windows 11.




DELL INSPIRON

Dell has packed everything (literally) into this all-in-one desktop. It cuts out the bulky tower for a sleek 23.8" Full HD monitor, which has all its bits and pieces inside. Its processor and RAM combo are great for basically anything - shopping, spreadsheets, Skype, the whole lot. So that’s your daily routine sorted. And don't worry about sorting a keyboard and mouse out - they're included in the box.




ACER ASPIRE C 27 ALL-IN-ONE

There's nothing better than settling back and watching the latest series. Enjoy it in all its glory on the large 27" Full HD screen – it's got IPS technology for vivid colours and wide viewing angles. It's great for calls too, and you'll look just as good thanks to the 5 MP webcam. Not ready to face your audience? Just slide the privacy cover across and you can make sure you won't be seen.




ASUS All In One - Intel® Core™

Fancy a compact workstation that won't clutter up your desk? This ASUS all-in-one PC is powered by the Intel® Core™ i3 processor – perfect for all your daily tasks, work, and entertainment. Its large Full HD touchscreen gives you vivid colours and wide viewing angles. And to match the visuals, there are bass-reflex speakers supporting Dolby Atmos, so you can enjoy your favourite show to the fullest after a hard day's work.
